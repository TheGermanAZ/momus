What is Momus?

Momus is a free Google plugin designed to detect sarcasm.

Get Extension

Problem

Individuals with autism spectrum disorders find it challenging to understand sarcasm, which is usually conveyed through tone, facial expression, and understanding of the topic being discussed. Instead, people with autism tend to take sarcastic speech literally, often leading to miscommunication and awkwardness. This is especially a problem in online spaces, where sarcasm is frequently used as a way of teasing and joking. It is harder for tone to come across correctly over text, and there is a lack of facial expressions that can aid understanding in real life.

Learn More

Solution

We created an extension called Momus (named after the Greek god of satire) that highlights all sarcastic text on a web page, to improve the understanding and clarity of online conversations for individuals with autism. We aim to reduce confusion and misinterpretation in order to faciliate better dialogue online.
